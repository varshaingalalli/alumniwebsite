Sir M. Visvesavaraya Block

The Vishveshwaraih hostel was started in the year 1968 to provide accommodation to the students of Basaveshwar Engineering College Bagalkot. The hostel has grown from its inception. Total rooms in the hostel are 112 with total capacity of 336.

Netaji Block

The Netaji hostel was started in the year 1996 to accommodate the increasing demand from the students of Basaveshwar Engineering College Bagalkot. This hostel was started to mainly to accommodate the first year students of the college. The hostel has a beautiful garden with all the modern facilities. Total rooms in the hostel are 100 with total capacity of 300 students.

Malaprabha Ladies Hostel

The hostel was started in the year 1998 to provide accommodation to the students of Basaveshwar Engineering College Bagalkot. The hostel has grown day by day from it inception. Total rooms available in the hostel are 190 with a total capacity of 468 students.

PG Boys Hostel

The hostel was started in the year 2013 to provide accommodation exclusively for PG and Research students of Basaveshwar Engineering College Bagalkot. Total rooms available in the hostel are 40 with a total capacity of 80 students.

    Canteen

    The college canteen is designed to cater to approximately 1000 students and all staff members. Our canteen has a modern kitchen.

    Transport

    The college provides transportation facility for students. There are sufficient buses, which take care of commutation of our students.

    Clinical Facility

    The campus has clinical facility, which is open to all the students and staff. The clinical facility has Two Qualified Medical Officers and a nursing staff.

    SPORTS

    The College is encouraging the sports activities amongst the students by providing the infrastructure and coaching facility under the guidance of Shri Ganesh Kori Physical Director, BEC Bagalkot. The BEC Cricket, Basketball, Badminton, Table Tennis, Hockey, Football, Carom and Chess teams have won many trophies, champion ships.

    Youth Red Cross Committee

    The College has Youth Red Cross Committee and organizes many social services.

    Industry on Campus

    Karnataka State Government IT, BT, S &T, Karnataka Biotechnology and Information Technology Services (KBITS) has set up Incubation Center at BEC campus to identify new technologies and software tools. Small scale industries are also planned for establishment inside the Campus with an aim to develop skills amongst students to meet industry demands.

    Internet Facilities

    BEC has separate becCampusLAN team to provide Internet facility throughout the campus. BEC has 100 Mbps Leased Line connectivity from BSNL.BEC is a Wi Fi enabled campus.
